How should we apply Neobrutalism to ChatFit?

Select a design direction for the landing page

The Full Brutalist (Loud & Aggressive)

Solid yellow hero section, thick black borders on everything, extreme contrast. The developer section gets a deep violet background.

B

The Minimal Brutalist (Clean & Focused)

Off-white background everywhere. Yellow and violet are used strictly for buttons and highlights. Thick borders and hard shadows on cards.

C

The Playful Brutalist (Balanced)

Alternating section colors (Off-white -> Yellow -> Violet). Fun offset shadows, flat colored cards, no gradients anywhere.
